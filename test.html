A site dedicated to raising awareness of and the support for a memorial to remember the little ones lost by abuse and neglect.

An average of four to seven children every day are lost to child abuse and neglect.

Help us lower this number by supporting our project!

Our Mission Statement

To remember the children who have died due to child abuse! By using this information, we will educate the public as to what to look for, what to listen for and who to contact in the event of a child needing your help. Through education we can help prevent child abuse and death due to child abuse. We will not stop until child abuse has been stopped.

Our Goal

To help educate people about the victims of child abuse and to raise money to create a national memorial for those who have been taken away from us by those who should have been their caregivers.
